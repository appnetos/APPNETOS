The APPNET OS Breadcrumbs app automatically generates breadcrumbs from the URI.
Simply installed and placed, the app creates a bar that is represented as arrows.
The links link to the respective child URIs from the Multilinugual URI management.
Background color, border color, font color, and link color are fully configurable in the administrator section of the app.

- Easy installation
- Automatically generate the breadcrumbs
- Colors fully configurable via the administrator section
- Container app. Size configurable via app settings
- Multilingual
- Administrator section in 6 languages. English, German, Spanish, French, Italian, Russian
- Multiple installation and configuration possible
- Free for commercial and non-commercial projects

Use:
Place in a suitable place, configure colors over the administrator area, done.
